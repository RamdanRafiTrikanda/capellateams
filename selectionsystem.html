Background

Shorinji Kempo is one of the martial arts originating from Japan.
Shorinji Kempo or commonly referred to as Kempo is generally seen as a
modified version of the martial art of Shaolinsi Kung Fu

The Shorinji Kempo sport has become a priority sport in East Kalimantan
because Kempo always achieves the medal target in every game. As for
the 2008 - 2021 PON, the Kempo sport has always maintained the overall champion

Challenge

However, based on temporary monitoring, currently the Shorinji Kempo
athlete selection is being carried out East Kalimantan is less effective and

efficient because it still uses the concept subjectivity that is using feelings. This happens because of factors the closeness of athletes to coaches or
referees causes jealousy between athlete.

What We Do

The first step we took after understanding the brief received was to make several designs such as Entity Relationship Diagrams (ERD), Sequence Diagrams and Class Diagrams.

After the design process has been carried out then enter the stage of making the program.

Easy to Use

We designed the user flow to be as easy to use as possible.

We use sequence diagrams in order to explain and display interactions between objects in a system in more detail.

Accurate and Valid Value

We make programs with valid and accurate values according to manual calculations that have been done by the client.

That way this program can help facilitate the selection of athletes.
