Small Team, Big Dreams

Capella is a team formed by Junico Tandiago, Arif Nurrohman, and Ramdan Rafi Trikanda with the main objective of assisting MSMEs and organizations in developing marketing and increasing their presence in the eyes of the public.

This team understands how important effective marketing is in business growth. Therefore, Capella is committed to providing assistance to MSMEs and organizations in developing appropriate marketing strategies to increase their visibility and brand awareness.

Capella takes a customer-oriented, results-focused approach. They provide various services including web/mobile design development as well as website-based applications.

Capella aims to help their clients grow and develop so that they can be recognized by more people and achieve greater success in the world of marketing.

What We Do

We understand and believe that in the development of application projects, good planning and processes will produce products that meet the needs and expectations of users.

1 - Discovery

The initial stage in our project development involves research to gain an in-depth understanding of user needs, project goals and business environment.

In this process, we will collect information, analyze challenges, and set strategic directions to produce a solid concept and become the basis for further development.

2 - Planning

Our second stage will be setting goals, determining the resources needed, making schedules, and identifying risks that might occur. With good planning, projects can be executed efficiently and achieve the expected results.

3 - Design

The third stage is the creative step in project development, where concepts and ideas are transformed into concrete designs.

From the design team will design the appearance, structure and functionality of the project according to user needs and preferences.

In this process, visual aspects and user experience are considered to produce an attractive and easy-to-use solution.

4 - Development

The fourth stage is the development process where the design and concept of the project is implemented into a real product.

The development team will use various technologies and programming languages to build the planned features.

In this process, program code is developed, testing is carried out, and iteration occurs to ensure the project runs as expected.

5 - Testing

The fifth stage is the testing process to test the application from several aspects such as functionality, performance and product reliability tested thoroughly.

Usually we hold joint meetings from the team (UI/UX, Frontend, Backend) to carry out tests to identify bugs, errors, and discrepancies with project requirements.

The test results are used as the basis for product improvements and improvements before being released to users.

6 - Launch

The sixth stage is the launch process which is the final stage in application development where the product is ready to be released to users.

The main goal is to introduce the app to the target audience, build awareness and invite users to use it. Launch also involves performance monitoring and feedback from users to continuously improve the user experience.
